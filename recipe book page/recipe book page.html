recipes

    chapati

  • add water
  • add salt
  • add whaeat flour
  • add 3 cups of hot water
  • mix the dough

    mahamri

  • add baking powder
  • add two table spoons of salt
  • add 4 table spoons of sugar
  • add one lemon
  • add wheat flour
  • then prepare the dough

    ugali

  • put a certain amount of water in a sufuria according to the size of ugali you are preparing
  • let the water boil
  • put 1 tablespoon of blueband and let it melt
  • add maize flour
  • then stir the mixture unytil there are no lumps
  • then you can serve your ugali with a stew of your choice or green vegetables

preparing boiled rice

  • add cooking oil to the sufuria
  • add onions
  • add 4 glasses of water
  • let the water boil
  • add 2 glasses of rice
  • then simmer so as toa void overcooking
  • then serve it with your favourite stew or curry.
